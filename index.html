🎮 PRIVACY POLICY

Anime Multiverse RNG Discord Bot

1. DATA COLLECTED

We collect and store the following information:

  • Your Discord ID and username
  • Your in-game inventory (characters, items, resources)
  • Your game statistics (level, progression, achievements)

2. USE OF DATA

The data is used exclusively to:

  • Save your in-game progress
  • Display leaderboards and player profiles
  • Enable player interactions (trades, co-op gameplay)

3. DATA SHARING

Your data is NEVER shared with third parties.

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to external parties. Your data remains secure within our game system.

4. SECURITY

All data is stored in a secure PostgreSQL database with industry-standard encryption and protection measures.

5. DATA DELETION

You have the right to request deletion of your data at any time. To do so, please contact us via:

  • Discord: Juro6666
  • Email: animemultiverserng@gmail.com

We will process your request within 30 days.

6. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy. Any changes will be announced in our Discord server.
