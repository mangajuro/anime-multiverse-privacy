⚔️ PRIVACY POLICY ⚔️

ANIME MULTIVERSE RNG

🎮 EFFECTIVE APRIL 7, 2026 🎮
1

📊 DATA COLLECTED

We collect and store the following information to provide our game services:

  • Your Discord ID and username (required for game identification)
  • Your in-game inventory (characters, items, resources, currencies)
  • Your game statistics (level, experience, progression, achievements)
  • Guild membership and contributions (if participating in guild features)
  • Game interaction history (rolls, battles, trades)
2

🎯 USE OF DATA

The collected data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Save and restore your in-game progress across sessions
  • Display leaderboards, rankings, and player profiles
  • Enable player interactions (trading, co-op gameplay, PvP battles)
  • Provide personalized game experiences and recommendations
  • Improve game balance and detect cheating or abuse
  • Send important game updates and announcements
3

🚫 DATA SHARING

🔒 YOUR DATA IS NEVER SHARED, SOLD, OR TRANSFERRED TO THIRD PARTIES!

We protect your privacy with the power of a thousand anime heroes!

We do not sell, trade, rent, or transfer your personal information to external parties for marketing or any other purposes. Your data remains secure within our game system and is only accessible to authorized game administrators for support purposes.

⚠️ Exception: We may disclose data only if required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users.

4

🛡️ SECURITY

All data is stored in a secure PostgreSQL database with industry-standard encryption and protection measures. We implement:

  • Encrypted database connections (SSL/TLS)
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication systems
  • Automated backups to prevent data loss

While we strive to protect your information with the strength of a legendary SSR character, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5

⏳ DATA RETENTION

We retain your game data as long as your account remains active. Inactive accounts (no activity for 12+ months) may have their data archived or deleted to maintain system performance. Like an abandoned anime protagonist's training ground!
